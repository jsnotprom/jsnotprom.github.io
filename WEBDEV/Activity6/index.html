Stardew Valley NPC's Gallery

Stardew Valley is a popular indie farming simulation role-playing game (RPG) developed by ConcernedApe (Eric Barone). It was first released in 2016 and has gained a large, dedicated fanbase thanks to its charming pixel art, relaxing gameplay, and deep content.In the game, you take the role of a character who inherits a dilapidated farm from their grandfather in Pelican Town. Your goal is to restore the farm to its former glory by growing crops, raising animals, and engaging with the local community. However, the game offers much more than just farming.
